About Me

I'm Agbetoye Oluwatoyin, an IBM Certified Data Scientist passionate about helping organizations make smarter decisions through data.

Hi, I'm Agbetoye "Toyin" Oluwatoyin. My interest in data comes from a simple idea: every business is generating valuable information, but most of it goes unused. I enjoy digging into data, finding the stories hidden inside it, and turning those insights into something useful, whether that's an interactive dashboard, a predictive model, or an automated workflow that saves hours of manual work.

Over the years, I've worked on projects involving business intelligence, machine learning, reporting automation, customer analytics, and performance tracking. I use tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel, Google Sheets, Zapier, and Typeform to build solutions that don't just look good, but actually help people make better decisions and work more efficiently. When I'm not building data solutions, I'm usually exploring new ways to combine analytics and automation to solve everyday business challenges.
